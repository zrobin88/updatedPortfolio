From Musician to Developer

Hello, my name is Zack Robin and I am a web developer, professional musician and producer. I graduated from Full Sail University with a Bachelor of Science in Audio Engineering and a Master of Science in Business. It was during my time at Full Sail that I began to take a profound interest in web development. Technology has been the primary disruptor of the music industry and always manages to set the stage for the way the industry operates, just look at Napster and the digital streaming revolution. This philosophy has served as the basis in the formation of my career path. Web and cloud-based technology has become the engine of music's progress and I strive to be on the forefront of such progress. My introduction to programing begain with an internship at a tech-startup company called Sirius IQ, where I learned the basics of coding, how development teams operate and utilizing the "scrum" project managmenet methodology.
